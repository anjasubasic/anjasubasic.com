About Me

My name is Anja (pronounced AHN-yuh) and I am originally from Belgrade, Serbia. I currently live in Joplin, MO, where I just graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.A. in Music Performance/Percussion. I currently work as a C# developer in the Joplin area, but I am enrolled in the CS M.S. program at Dartmouth for Fall 2017 and I will be moving to Hanover on September 1, which I am very excited about!

Experience

I spent most of my time there working on a Windows Store application and going through C# training.
